About Us

At Vionlux Lighting Design & Consultancy, we believe that lighting is more than just illumination — it’s an art that shapes mood, enhances functionality, and transforms spaces. With a passion for creativity and a commitment to precision, we craft bespoke lighting solutions tailored to each client’s unique vision. Whether it’s a warm, inviting home, a vibrant commercial space, or a striking outdoor ambiance, our designs balance aesthetics, efficiency, and innovation. From concept to installation, we work closely with you to ensure every detail shines — bringing your spaces to life through the perfect harmony of light and design. At VIONLUX, we create lighting solutions that enhance spaces, elevate functionality, and inspire. With expertise in residential, commercial, facade and landscape projects, we blend technical precision with creative vision to deliver lighting that transforms environments. Our work is guided by Functionality, Aesthetics, and Sustainability, ensuring the perfect balance of light, comfort, and efficiency. From energy-efficient designs to advanced control systems, we bring your vision to light — with brilliance.
